SubGenius: The All-in-One Saas Solution for B2B Startups

The Challenge

B2B startups with subscription based revenue models face significant hurdles in managing their Subscriptions and accurately predicting future revenue. Inefficiencies, revenue leakage and poor decision making can hinder growth and profitability.

Our Mission

At SubGenius, we aim to empower B2B startups by providing a comprehensive SaaS tool that simplifies subscription management and enhances revenue forecasting. Our goal is to reduce manual workload and increase accuracy in revenue projections.

Our Solution

SubGenius is designed to address the challenges faced by B2B startups with subscriptionbased models. Our platform automates subscription billing, tracks customer renewals and leverage historical data to generate precise revenue forecasts.
